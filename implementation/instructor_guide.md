# Instructor Guide

## Getting Started

You do not need to redesign your entire course.

Start by:
- Adding reflection prompts
- Including AI disclosure forms
- Requesting draft submissions
- Evaluating reasoning alongside correctness

## Recommended First Steps

1. Add one reflective component
2. Introduce process-tracking gradually
3. Use lightweight rubrics
4. Encourage transparent AI usage

## Goal

The objective is not to ban AI usage, but to better understand authentic learning processes.
